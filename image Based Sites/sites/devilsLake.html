Devils lake is awesome. The first time we went to the lake we didn't have swimming clothes, but we still wanted to kayak, so we did. That first time was amazing, I had never been kayaking before so it was awesome to just paddle along in the water with my family. After that I knew the next time we went I wanted to record all that I could. So when we did go for a second time I was ready with water proof phone cases for everyone. I captured a POV shot of me just pattling, to some stunning underwater videos that are amazingly clear.
