Baraboo is a very important place in my family. We have gone camping there so many times over the years that I've lost track. Everytime we go we do something different, either we find a new hiking area, or we finally go kayaking over the lake, we always try to do something new and fun. Baraboo also has a lot of history in my family, Its the first place my father and mother went camping together, and years later its where my father proposed to my mother. Its a fantastic area to go camping with tons of scenery and endless things to do.
