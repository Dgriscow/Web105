Introduction

Nature is everywhere, you can't escape it. For as long as humans have been around, we have wandered through vast forests in search of something. Nowadays we have forests of concrete surrounding us, so sometimes it's good to take a vacation back to the forests. This website is a portfolio of some of the various forests I've been to. My family really enjoys going on hiking trips, especially baraboo and to Devils lake. I've also been to various other forests, one of them being starved rock, that was super fun. Then there are also a few more places I would like to visit, and some of them aren't necessarily forests, but they are still great places to visit.
